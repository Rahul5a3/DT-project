Defense:

If you are patriotic and are eager to serve the country, then you can join the Indian Army or Navy, or Air Force after B.Tech. Indian army has a highly skilled technical wing that supports the troops on the field. Thus, there are many opportunities for engineers in the technical wings of the Indian army.


lets some ways for Engineering Graduates to join in Indian Army,Navy,Air forces:



Indian Army:

Type Of Entry Age Qualification Mode Of Selection
University Entry Scheme 19-25 years Engineering Degree Course Campus Interview and SSB Interview
TGC(Engineering Graduates) 20-27 years Engineering Degree in notified discipline Final and Pre-final year students SSB Interview
Short Service Commission (Technical) 20-27 years Engineering Degree in notified discipline SSB Interview
Short Service Commission (Women Tech-Nontechnical) 19-25 years(Non-tech) Graduates / Post Graduates / LLB / Engineering SSB Interview

Indian Navy:

Type Of Entry Age Qualification Mode Of Selection
University Entry Scheme (SSC) 19 - 24 years Final Year students of Marine / Mech/Aeronautical/Aerospace/Automotive/ Control/Industrial/Metallurgy/Mechatronics/ Production Engineering with minimum 60% upto 6th Semester. SSB Interview
Short Service Commission 19½ - 25 years BE / BTech in Marine / Mech / Aeronautical / Control / Metallurgical / Production Engineering with minimum 60% marks SSB Interview
Short Service Commission (Submarine) 19½ - 25 years BE / BTech in Mechanical Engineering with minimum 60% marks. SSB Interview
Short Service Commission Entry 21 - 25 years BE/BTech in Naval Architecture / Mech/Civil/ Aeronautical / Metallurgical /Aerospace Engg with 60% marks. SSB Interview
University Entry Scheme (SSC) 19 - 24 years BE/BTech in Naval Architecture / Mech/Civil/ Aeronautical / Metallurgical /Aerospace Engg with 60% marks SSB Interview
Special Naval Architect Entry Scheme (SNAES) 21 - 25 Yrs BE/BTech in Naval Architecture with 60% marks (campus recruitment). SSB Interview
